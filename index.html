LOOKING FOR A BIRTHDAY PARTY VENUE IN THE COASTAL BEND?

If you are looking for a great Corpus Christi birthday party location for your child, check out Funtrackers. We are one of the best Coastal Bend birthday venues because we offer so much for kids. You can plan with confidence because we have over 500 reviews averaging 4.4 out of 5 stars from Facebook and Google. The key to a great birthday is great food, lots of rides and go karts, big fun like batting cages and bungee and a great birthday cake. What makes for the best birthday parties in Corpus Christi? The birthday parties last for hours where the kids just don’t want to go like you will find at Funtrackers – Just read our hundreds of reviews.

There is a full arcade including cranes with prizes and the latest high-speed chasers, along with three different ways to ride go karts – the grand prix, the family track and the rookie track. Everyone loves bumper cars and ours are great! Test your skill on our newly-remodeled 18-hole mini golf course and in the exciting bungee dome. We have state of the art batting cages with 7 stalls where you can choose your speed and pitches. There is also a ropes course and many different rides available. You could spend all day at Funtrackers and that is why it makes such a great Corpus Christi birthday place!

What about the hours you can plan your child’s birthday? On Fridays and Saturdays the full Padre Island area arcade and rides are open from 11 AM to 11 PM.
